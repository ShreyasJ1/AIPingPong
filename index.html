Developed by - Shreyas

Instructions:


1. Keep your device screen straight

2. Move yourself approximately 3 - 4 feet away from your device

3. Move your right wrist up and down.A red dot should appear on your right wrist

4. Press the play button and again, move yourself approximately 3 - 4 feet away from your device


The red paddle is your paddle.It will move according to the movement of your wrist (up and down).

A red dot will appear on your right wrist if it is detected.
